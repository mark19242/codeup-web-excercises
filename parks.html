National Parks Facts

Sequoia National Park

  • Established: September 25, 1890
  • Location: California
  • Size: 404,064 acres
  • Features: Giant sequoia trees, Moro Rock, Crystal Cave

Mount Rainier National Park

  • Established: March 2, 1899
  • Location: Washington
  • Size: 236,381 acres
  • Features: Mount Rainier, subalpine meadows, Paradise area

Crater Lake National Park

  • Established: May 22, 1902
  • Location: Oregon
  • Size: 183,224 acres
  • Features: Crater Lake, Wizard Island, Old Man of the Lake
